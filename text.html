i. an introduction

To carve your life

From the earth beneath you

From the air around you

As its quality dilutes

And what you view

From your eyes that cannot see

Clearly

Who do you want to be on an Earth -

That spits you from its core

Who were you before?

The glow of the world was taken away

From the circumstances of your birth it was never meant to stay and you never were formed to have a say

To carve your life from everything that was never yours

Is to make it known that it was meant to be

From the earth beneath you, from the air around you

As its quality dilutes

From your eyes that try to see clearly

Who do you want to be
