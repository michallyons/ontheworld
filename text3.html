iii. to before

Further than was in your grasp

The quality of life

Escaped you from the moment that

Birth had come upon you

Through the eyes of the new

The idea was in mind

That there would not be a time where this was not meant for you, too

From circumstance it quickly turned

That the concept could not stay

Taken away was the shroud
