ii. who dilutes?

Before there was that form of yours

The world has been turning

Labored on - by necessity, or so is said, or used to be

Now so to an end

What goal is in sight

Whose?

It is your turn to take up the chisel (as has been done by many before)

Is it for you? The world? Or the so-said world?

Supposedly, you’ll get to choose

To get to the top of the world is to change it

Again, to what end?

And to this end, what do you see when you get there?

At that end, you are there with who?

The so-said world

The world

You

What’s to be said of what’s said to be beneath you?

Will the world keep turning?
