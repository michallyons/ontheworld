iv. clearly

Are you yet to decide what can change?

When you were shot through the crust of conscious

How it interplays with a conscience that was built from a miracle

So said is that if you climb up to the top of the Earth then you’ll have earned it

Ask what does it mean to earn

Is the ground from the so-called bottom not the same as the ground at the top?

Each day the Earth turns, each day then becomes before

And from before, you’ll have learned was was built, prior to the crash of quality

That what the world could be what you’d like it to be

But how is above you
